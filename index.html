Transmission 2026.02.07: Logistics

Greetings traveler. As we approach the date of our rendezvous, please note the following:

Plus One

If it was not explicitly communicated previously, your invite is valid for you plus one. Feel free to bring a friend or loved one if they can stomach the nerdery—or if they can hold their own in Sabacc, of course.

Refreshments

Spotchka, revnog, and a small selection of ale and non-alcoholic drinks will be available for all guests. Light snacks and dessert will also be served. If there is a particular drink or snack you would prefer, feel free to bring it with you.

Games

Sabacc is often a gambling card game, but we’ll be playing for bragging rights more than anything. There’s no buy-in or betting involved.

If you would like to brush up on the game before the party, you can play on your datapad. Specifically, we will be playing Kessel Sabacc. Cards, shift tokens, and chips will be provided by the house.

Attire

Lastly, while these transmissions and the party environment itself may be fairly immersive, costumes are not expected. Dress however you feel comfortable for an indoor party with friends!
